BlockRider

 BlockRider is a fast, fun and challenging game for iPhone and iPad.

the game is very simple, tap the screen to start the ball moving and then tap it again to change direction. That's it, but don't fall off the Blocks!

There are Power-Ups to help you along the way as well as 3 rides at the start.

In the Settings you can choose from 3 different speeds, Slow, Hyper-Active and Ludicrous, and three different colour schemes, GonetoPlaid Blue, Pink or Random

Finally you can choose to have the music sound track and sound FX on or off

For the Young Riders, there is a Kids Mode in settings as well, which slows the game down and makes it a bit easier. No Adults allowed!

Can you be the Champion BlockRider and head the leaderboard?

Download for free from the App Store now!
