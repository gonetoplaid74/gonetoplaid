List!

List! is a shopping list app that works with the whole family

Each group has 4 shared lists, and as soon as one member of the family adds something to the list it is visible to everyone in the group

You can also use the app as a private group chat tool. No one can see the chat but the members of the group

The top list is designed for groceries, and has an aisle field to make shopping and sorting of the list easier.

The other lists can be used for anthing you want and can be renamed in settings

There are also two private lists, only visible on the device of the person who adds to them, perfect for gifts and personal notes.

Please see our Instruction Manual, for full details of how to use the app.
