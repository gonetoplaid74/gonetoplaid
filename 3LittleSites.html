3LittleSites

3LittleSites is an ultra-fast, minimalist web browser

There is no history, no plugins, no bookmarks, in short nothing to slow down your browsing experience

When the app first loads you are presented with the option to enter three of your favourite websites and store them

The chosen websites are accessed from shortcuts at the bottom of the screen. Also included are back and forward navigation buttons and a reload button, and finally a bookmark button so you can update and store your favourites at any time. Thatâ€™s it!

The result is a virtually full screen web browser that loads and displays web pages faster than anything else available
